About Me

I am passionate about cybersecurity and committed to becoming a skilled cybersecurity analyst.

Currently, I am pursuing a Diplôme d'études collégiales (DEC) in Computer Science – Network Infrastructure and Security at Collège de Maisonneuve, where I am gaining in-depth knowledge of IT systems, network security, and infrastructure management.

I am always eager to connect with professionals in the field, learn from mentors, and contribute to meaningful projects that expand my expertise.
